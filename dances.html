Reading These Visualizations

These cyberspace renderings translate motion-capture point clouds into navigable 3D environments. The geometric forms are not interpretations—they are direct mappings of captured joint positions over time. What you're seeing is the literal shape that movement makes in space.

Use your mouse to orbit and zoom. Notice how different traditions create different spatial signatures: the vertical emphasis of Irish step, the horizontal expansions of Ewe warrior dances, the spiraling paths of Orisha embodiment. These geometries are cultural fingerprints.

For comparative analysis, use the Compare tool to view two dances side by side and explore structural relationships across traditions.
